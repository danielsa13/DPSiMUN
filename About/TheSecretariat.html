The Secretariat

Executive Overview

The Secretariat is the backbone of the Model United Nations, responsible for the logistical and substantive preparation of the conference. They ensure that every committee operates smoothly and that every delegate has the resources needed for a successful debate.

Administrative Duties

From managing registrations to overseeing the publication of study guides, the Secretariat handles the administrative complexities that define a high-standard MUN experience. Their goal is to maintain the academic integrity and professional atmosphere of the entire event.

Leadership and Coordination

Headed by the Secretary-General, the team coordinates with various departments, including media, finance, and security, to provide a seamless transition from the opening ceremony to the final resolutions.
