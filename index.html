About the Conference

A forum for the next generation of global leaders

Greenfield MUN brings together high school delegates from across the region for three days of debate, collaboration, and diplomacy. Delegates research real-world issues, represent assigned nations, and draft resolutions — developing skills in public speaking, negotiation, and international relations.

Whether you are a first-time delegate or a veteran chair, GAMUN offers a rigorous and welcoming environment to engage with some of the world's most pressing challenges.
